Delete your Auros account

Auros, by Meridian

You can delete your Auros account and everything associated with it at any time. You only have an account if you chose to sign in; Auros works fully without one.

Delete it in the app (fastest)

  1. Open Auros.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Scroll to Account.
  4. Tap Delete account and confirm.

This takes effect immediately and cannot be undone.

Can't access the app?

If you can no longer open the app, request deletion through meridian.novalabsos.com using the email address on your account. We will delete the account and its data within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and sign-in (your email address or Google sign-in).
  • Everything synced to Meridian's cloud: your saved locations and app settings.

What is kept

Nothing about your account is kept on our servers after deletion, and there is no additional retention period. Any data stored only on your device (for example, locations saved while you were signed out) is removed when you uninstall Auros or clear the app's data on that device.

Questions

Reach Meridian through meridian.novalabsos.com.
