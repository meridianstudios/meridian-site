WHAT IT IS

The whole desktop, reimagined.

Nova OS is Meridian's take on the desktop — not just a theme, but a complete, cohesive environment. A calm cosmic interface, a glanceable menu bar, and a frosted dock that keeps your apps a tap away. It's designed so the computer gets out of your way and lets you get to work.

Built on modern web technology, Nova OS stays fast and lightweight while feeling unmistakably polished — every corner, animation, and color considered.
