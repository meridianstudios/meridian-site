WHAT IT IS

The whole desktop, reimagined.

Nova OS is Meridian's take on the desktop — not a theme, but a complete, cohesive environment. A calm cosmic interface, a glanceable menu bar, and a frosted dock keep your apps a tap away, so the computer gets out of your way and lets you work.

It runs on a native core with modern web tech, so it stays fast and lightweight while feeling unmistakably polished — every corner, animation, and color considered.
